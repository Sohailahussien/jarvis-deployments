## How to Use This Kit

1. **Review the Customer Profile** - Understand who you're demoing to
2. **Select Relevant Use Cases** - Choose 3-4 scenarios that match their challenges
3. **Load the Synthetic Data** - Upload CSV files to facilis.ai workspace
4. **Run Demo Prompts** - Copy-paste prompts and let facilis.ai work
5. **Show Sample Apps** - Demonstrate full application building capability
6. **Address Objections** - Use prepared responses confidently

## Quick Start for First Demo

For your first demo, we recommend this 20-minute flow:

1. **Hook (2 min)**: Start with water loss statistics in GCC
2. **Demo 1 (5 min)**: Water quality analysis with synthetic data
3. **Demo 2 (5 min)**: Distribution network optimization
4. **Demo 3 (5 min)**: Build a simple dashboard app live
5. **Close (3 min)**: ROI calculation and next steps
