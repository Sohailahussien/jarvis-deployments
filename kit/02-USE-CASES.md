# Water Utilities Use Case Scenarios

## Overview

This document outlines 7 comprehensive use cases demonstrating how facilis.ai solves critical challenges faced by water utilities in the GCC region. Each scenario includes the business challenge, facilis.ai solution, expected outcomes, and implementation timeline.

---

## Use Case 1: Non-Revenue Water (NRW) Reduction

### Business Challenge
Water utilities in the GCC lose 13-35% of treated water through distribution inefficiencies (leaks, theft, meter inaccuracies). For a mid-sized utility producing 50 million gallons/day, this represents $15-40M in annual losses.

**Specific Pain Points:**
- Unable to pinpoint leak locations quickly
- Reactive maintenance approach (fix after failure)
- Limited visibility into pressure zones
- Manual inspection processes miss early warnings
- Lack of historical trend analysis

### facilis.ai Solution

**Phase 1: Data Integration (Week 1)**
- Connect SCADA systems, flow meters, pressure sensors
- Integrate GIS data for pipe network mapping
- Import historical maintenance records
- Consolidate customer meter readings

**Phase 2: AI Analysis (Week 2-3)**
- Analyze 2+ years of pressure/flow data
- Identify anomaly patterns indicating leaks
- Map high-risk zones based on pipe age, material, soil conditions
- Correlate weather data with failure incidents
- Build predictive leak detection model

**Phase 3: Dashboard & Alerts (Week 4)**
- Real-time NRW monitoring dashboard
- Automated alerts for pressure anomalies
- Prioritized repair list by estimated water loss
- Mobile app for field crews with exact locations
- ROI tracking per repair completed

### Expected Outcomes

**Financial Impact:**
- 40-60% reduction in NRW within 6 months
- $6-24M annual savings for 50M gal/day utility
- Payback period: 2-4 months

**Operational Impact:**
- 75% faster leak detection (days to hours)
- 50% reduction in emergency repairs
- 30% improvement in crew efficiency
- 90% reduction in manual inspection time

**Environmental Impact:**
- Millions of gallons conserved annually
- Reduced energy consumption (less water to treat/pump)
- Extended infrastructure lifespan

### Implementation Timeline
- **Week 1**: Data integration and validation
- **Week 2**: Historical analysis and model training
- **Week 3**: Dashboard development and testing
- **Week 4**: User training and rollout
- **Ongoing**: Continuous model improvement

### Demo Data Required
- Pressure sensor readings (15-minute intervals)
- Flow meter data by zone
- Pipe network GIS data
- Maintenance history
- Weather data

## Use Case 2: Energy Consumption Optimization

### Business Challenge
Desalination and water pumping are extremely energy-intensive. Saudi Arabia alone consumes 300,000 barrels of oil daily for desalination. Energy costs typically represent 30-50% of operational expenses for water utilities.

**Specific Pain Points:**
- No visibility into energy consumption patterns
- Unable to correlate energy use with production/demand
- Peak demand charges add 20-30% to energy bills
- Equipment runs inefficiently (pumps, motors)
- Manual process for energy reporting

### facilis.ai Solution

**Phase 1: Energy Data Integration**
- Connect energy management systems (EMS)
- Integrate production data (water output)
- Link weather data (temperature affects demand)
- Import tariff structure and billing data
- Add equipment specifications (pumps, motors, RO units)

**Phase 2: AI-Powered Analysis**
- Identify energy waste patterns
- Correlate consumption with production efficiency
- Detect equipment operating outside optimal parameters
- Analyze peak demand patterns
- Model scenarios for load shifting

**Phase 3: Optimization Recommendations**
- Real-time energy dashboard by system/equipment
- Automated recommendations for efficiency improvements
- Load shifting opportunities to avoid peak charges
- Equipment performance benchmarking
- Predictive alerts for declining efficiency

### Expected Outcomes

**Financial Impact:**
- 15-25% reduction in energy costs
- $3-7M annual savings for typical desalination plant
- Additional 10-15% savings from peak demand reduction
- ROI: 3-6 months

**Operational Impact:**
- Real-time visibility into energy consumption
- Automated compliance reporting
- 60% reduction in time spent on energy management
- Data-driven equipment upgrade decisions

**Sustainability Impact:**
- Significant carbon footprint reduction
- Support for renewable energy integration
- Improved sustainability reporting

### Implementation Timeline
- **Week 1**: Energy system integration
- **Week 2**: Historical analysis and baseline establishment
- **Week 3**: Optimization model development
- **Week 4**: Dashboard and recommendation engine
- **Ongoing**: Continuous optimization

### Demo Data Required
- Hourly energy consumption by system
- Production output data
- Equipment operational parameters

- Weather/temperature data

## Use Case 3: Predictive Maintenance for Critical Assets

### Business Challenge
Water utilities manage thousands of critical assets (pumps, valves, motors, pipes) with limited maintenance budgets. Reactive maintenance leads to unexpected failures, service disruptions, and 3-5x higher repair costs compared to planned maintenance.

**Specific Pain Points:**
- No early warning of impending failures
- Over-maintain some assets, under-maintain others
- Emergency repairs disrupt service and cost premium
- Limited maintenance budget requires prioritization
- Lack of asset performance history

### facilis.ai Solution

**Phase 1: Asset Data Integration**
- Import CMMS (maintenance management system) data
- Connect IoT sensors (vibration, temperature, flow)
- Integrate asset registry with specifications
- Load historical failure records
- Add operational hours/cycles tracking

**Phase 2: Predictive Models**
- Analyze failure patterns by asset type
- Build remaining useful life (RUL) models
- Correlate operational conditions with failures
- Identify early warning indicators
- Risk scoring for each critical asset

**Phase 3: Maintenance Optimization**
- Prioritized maintenance schedule dashboard
- Automated work order generation
- Spare parts inventory optimization
- Mobile app for maintenance crews
- ROI tracking per prevented failure

### Expected Outcomes

**Financial Impact:**
- 40-60% reduction in emergency repairs
- 25-35% lower overall maintenance costs
- $2-5M annual savings for mid-sized utility
- 3x reduction in unplanned downtime costs
- ROI: 4-8 months

**Operational Impact:**
- 70% of failures prevented before occurrence
- 50% improvement in maintenance crew productivity
- 90% reduction in service disruptions
- Optimized spare parts inventory (20-30% reduction)

**Service Quality Impact:**
- 95%+ service reliability
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Extended asset lifespans (15-25%)

### Implementation Timeline
- **Week 1-2**: Asset and sensor data integration
- **Week 3**: Historical failure analysis
- **Week 4**: Predictive model development
- **Week 5**: Dashboard and workflow automation
- **Week 6**: User training and pilot program
- **Ongoing**: Model refinement and expansion

### Demo Data Required
- Asset inventory with specifications
- Maintenance history (repairs, replacements)
- Sensor data (vibration, temperature, pressure)
- Operational hours/cycles
- Failure incident reports

## Use Case 5: Customer Demand Forecasting

### Business Challenge
Water utilities struggle to accurately predict demand, leading to over-production (wasted energy/costs) or under-production (service pressure issues). Traditional forecasting methods are simplistic and don't account for multiple influencing factors.

**Specific Pain Points:**
- Static demand forecasts (seasonal averages)
- Unable to predict impact of weather, events, holidays
- Over-reliance on safety buffers (expensive)
- Production planning based on guesswork
- Reservoir management inefficiencies

### facilis.ai Solution

**Phase 1: Demand Data Integration**
- Import 3+ years of hourly consumption data
- Integrate weather data (temperature, humidity)
- Add calendar data (holidays, events, Ramadan)
- Include population/demographic trends
- Link industrial customer production schedules

**Phase 2: AI Forecasting Models**
- Multi-variable demand prediction (hourly, daily, weekly)
- Weather impact modeling
- Special event demand adjustment
- Long-term growth forecasting
- Confidence intervals and scenario planning

**Phase 3: Production Optimization**
- Demand forecast dashboard (7-day, 30-day, 1-year)
- Optimized production schedules
- Reservoir level management
- Energy cost minimization (produce during off-peak)
- Automated alerts for forecast deviations

### Expected Outcomes

**Financial Impact:**
- 10-20% reduction in production costs
- Optimized energy consumption (off-peak production)
- $1-3M annual savings for mid-sized utility
- Reduced infrastructure investment (accurate growth planning)

**Operational Impact:**
- 85-95% forecast accuracy
- Optimized reservoir utilization
- Reduced emergency production events
- Better staff/resource planning

**Service Quality Impact:**
- Consistent water pressure
- Reduced service interruptions
- Improved customer satisfaction

### Implementation Timeline
- **Week 1-2**: Historical data collection and preparation
- **Week 3**: Model development and training
- **Week 4**: Forecast dashboard development
- **Week 5**: Production optimization integration
- **Ongoing**: Model accuracy monitoring

### Demo Data Required
- Hourly/daily consumption history (3+ years)
- Weather data (temperature, rainfall)
- Holiday/event calendar
- Population growth data
- Major customer usage patterns
